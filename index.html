Who We Are

A One-Stop Trading Partner for Global Markets

Vagmi Exim is an import, export and trading company with deep roots in Latur, Maharashtra. We specialise in a wide range of goods — from coal and minerals to agricultural grains, dehydrated vegetable powders, animal feed and Indian spices — serving buyers and sellers across multiple geographies.

Our commitment is simple: transparent transactions, consistent quality, and long-term partnerships built on trust.

Document Flow — Mineral Transactions

A clear four-phase process that protects both buyer and seller from negotiation to payment.

01
Negotiation
  • Request for Offer & LOI
  • Specification & Certificate of Analysis
  • Full Corporate Offer (FCO)
  • Price & Conditions agreed
  • Site Inspection
→
02
Contract
  • ICPO issued
  • Contract Signed
  • LC / SBLC / BG Applied
  • Surveyor / Inspection Assigned
  • MV & Barge Nominations

03
Delivery
